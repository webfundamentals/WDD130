Frequently Asked Questions

This page involves answers to the questions frequently asked by the customers.

• Where is your store located?

We are located along 1st Rd. Brgy. Villamonte, Bacolod City. If you’re going from Lopues-East to STI - West Negros University, you can find us on the left side across the XYZ hardware. Besides our cafe, there is Iris and Yuri’s store. Just feel free to come and visit us!

• What are your best-sellers?

Our menu features a wide array of delectable options, but one of our undeniable crowd-pleasers is our signature "Halo-halo." This beloved Filipino dessert is a true masterpiece, artfully crafted with layers of sweet beans, colorful gelatin, fresh fruits, and shaved ice, all crowned with a luscious scoop of ice cream and a drizzle of sweetened condensed milk. It's a customer favorite and a must-try for those looking to indulge in the perfect blend of flavors and textures.
