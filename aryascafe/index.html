About Us

Arya’s Cafe serves a wide variety of beverages in a relaxed and casual setting. In addition to our selection of drinks, we also offer light meals and snacks. This may include desserts, sandwiches, and other small, easy-to-prepare dishes. In our store, you can enjoy a relaxed and informal atmosphere where you can socialize, work, read, or simply enjoy a relaxed moment. You may visit us along 1st Rd. Brgy. Villamonte, Bacolod City. We’re open for extended hours, including early mornings and late evenings, to accommodate our various customer schedules. Aside from Arya’s Cafe, you can also check the products from Iris and Yuri’s store.
